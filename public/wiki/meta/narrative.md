# Lakshveer Rao — Narrative

## Summary

Lakshveer Rao (born ~2009, age 16-17 as of 2026) is an Indian maker, hardware entrepreneur, and AI builder from Hyderabad. He has documented 170+ electronics and robotics projects on YouTube since childhood, sold 300+ units of CircuitHeroes (an educational electronics card game), and recently shipped MotionX and Drishtikon Yantra — computer vision systems for gesture recognition and assistive technology. Notable for national recognition (PARAM Award, Malpani Foundation grant) and participation in high-selectivity hackathons (Scaler Innovation Lab finals, Polaris VibeHack) before age 10.
