# Lakshveer Rao — Narrative
*Meta Article*

## Summary

Lakshveer Rao (born 2018) is an Indian hardware maker and entrepreneur from Hyderabad. At age 8, he is India's youngest hardware startup founder, operating **Projects by Laksh** and shipping commercial products including **CircuitHeroes** (educational electronics card game, 300+ decks sold) and **Drishtikon Yantra** (Raspberry Pi-based assistive vision device). He has documented 170+ builds on YouTube since age 4 [[sig-first-yt-upload-2022]], demonstrated projects to ISRO Chairman Shri S. Somanath [[sig-isro-meeting-2025]], and received recognition from Malpani Foundation (₹1,00,000 grant), Param Foundation, and The Residency Delta-2 cohort (youngest founder ever selected) [[sig-delta2-residency-2025]]. His work spans electronics, robotics, computer vision, and AI agents.
