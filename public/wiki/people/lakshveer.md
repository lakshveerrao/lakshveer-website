# Lakshveer Rao

*8-year-old Indian hardware founder, maker, and youngest hardware startup founder in India*

## Summary

Lakshveer Rao (age 8, Grade 2, Hyderabad) is India's youngest hardware startup founder and co-founder of **Projects by Laksh**. He began building at age 4 with his first YouTube upload [[sig-first-yt-upload-2022]] and first DC motor fan [[sig-first-dc-motor-2022]]. By age 8, he has documented 170+ electronics, robotics, and AI projects on YouTube [[sig-yt-170-projects-2025]], created **CircuitHeroes** (educational electronics card game with 300+ decks sold) [[sig-circuitheroes-launch-2024]], and built AI agents controlling hardware via Telegram [[sig-fe-mait-agent-2026]]. He has presented to ISRO Chairman S. Somanath [[sig-isro-meeting-2025]], participated in 15+ hackathons (finalist/winner in multiple), received grants from Malpani Foundation [[sig-malpani-grant-2025]] and AI Grants India [[sig-ai-grants-india-2025]], and was selected for The Residency Delta-2 accelerator [[sig-delta2-residency-2025]] — the youngest founder ever in the program.
