## Summary

Lakshveer's electronics practice spans hardware builds, PCB design, IoT systems, and AI-hardware integration. Starting with a DC motor fan at age 4 [[sig-first-dc-motor-2022]], he has documented 170+ projects on YouTube [[sig-yt-170-projects-2025]] and demonstrated work to ISRO Chairman S. Somanath [[sig-isro-meeting-2025]]. His electronics work bridges education (CircuitHeroes card game), competitive hardware (Lion Circuits hackathons), and AI agent control systems (Telegram-ESP32 integration).
