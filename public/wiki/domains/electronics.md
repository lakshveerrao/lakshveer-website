Lakshveer's electronics practice began at age 4 (2022-07) with basic hardware builds and has evolved into advanced IoT, robotics, and AI-hardware integration systems. His work spans 170+ documented projects, hackathon wins as youngest participant, demonstrations to ISRO leadership, and educational products like CircuitHeroes.
