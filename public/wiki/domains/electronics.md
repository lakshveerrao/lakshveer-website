# electronics
*Domain*

## Summary

Lakshveer began building electronics at age 4 with a DC motor fan [[sig-first-dc-motor-2022]] and has documented 170+ projects on YouTube [[sig-yt-170-projects-2025]]. His work spans IoT, robotics, PCB design, and AI-hardware integration. He demonstrated electronics to ISRO Chairman S. Somanath [[sig-isro-meeting-2025]], competed in Lion Circuits hackathons as youngest participant [[sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025]], and secured a co-creation agreement with Lion Circuits [[sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025]]. Key output: **CircuitHeroes** educational card game (300+ decks sold, ₹1L Malpani grant [[sig-malpani-grant-2025]]) and **Hardvare** platform merging hardware with AI agents [[sig-hardvare-system-2026]].
