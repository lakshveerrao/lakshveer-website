# electronics

## Summary

Electronics represents Lakshveer Rao's foundational technical domain, spanning hardware prototyping, embedded systems (ESP32), and physical computing interfaces. His work bridges traditional circuit design with AI agent systems, demonstrating capability from basic project builds to hardware-software integration that earned national recognition and ISRO leadership attention.
