# electronics
*Domain*

## Summary

Lakshveer Rao has been building electronics projects since age 4, starting with a DC motor fan in 2022-08 [[sig-first-dc-motor-2022]]. By 2025-12, he documented 170+ builds on YouTube spanning robotics, IoT, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and AI-controlled hardware [[sig-yt-170-projects-2025]]. He demonstrated electronics to ISRO Chairman S. Somanath in 2025-04 [[sig-isro-meeting-2025]], taught IoT at IIT Hyderabad in 2025-02 [[sig-iit-hyd-iot-workshop-2025]], and signed a co-creation agreement with Lion Circuits (PCB manufacturer) in 2025-11 [[sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025]]. His work bridges physical hardware, AI agents, and education through both hands-on builds and his CircuitHeroes card game.
