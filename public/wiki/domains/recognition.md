## Summary

Lakshveer Rao has received recognition from startup ecosystem leaders, hackathon organizers, media outlets, and technical communities since late 2024. Notable milestones include endorsements from Param Foundation and Runable leadership, feature articles positioning him as India's youngest hardware founder, demonstrations to former ISRO Chairman S. Somanath, and consistent recognition as "youngest builder" at technical events. Recognition spans four categories: peer/mentor endorsements, media features, institutional spotlights, and event-based visibility.
