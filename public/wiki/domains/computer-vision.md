## Summary

Computer vision represents a core technical domain in Lakshveer's work, spanning assistive technology and human-computer interaction. His projects demonstrate practical applications from accessibility solutions for the visually impaired to gesture-based AI control systems, showing progression from hardware-integrated vision systems to pure AI-based recognition models.

## Evidence

Lakshveer has shipped multiple computer vision products between 2025-2026. **Drishtikon Yantra** ([[drishtikon-yantra]]) is an assistive vision device designed for visually impaired users, combining computer vision with hardware integration (2026-01). **MotionX** ([[motionx]]) is an AI gesture recognition system that enables hands-free computer control through visual input, launched January 2026. His South Park Commons pitch demo (August 2025) featured Drishtikon Yantra as a flagship project, indicating early public validation of his vision work. A YouTube Short demonstration (March 2026) showcased integration capabilities with Telegram agents, suggesting continued expansion of vision applications.
