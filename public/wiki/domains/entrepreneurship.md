## Summary

Lakshveer Rao began formal entrepreneurial activity at age 7 with his first business pitch for DIY Cars [[sig-first-pitch-2024](2024-03)]. By age 8, he had launched multiple revenue-generating products (CircuitHeroes card game, DIY eBook), secured institutional grants (Malpani Foundation ₹1,00,000, AI Grants India), and entered competitive accelerators (The Residency Delta-2 as youngest founder ever selected). His entrepreneurial trajectory spans hardware products, educational content, and AI agents — with consistent public presence at tier-1 Indian startup events.
