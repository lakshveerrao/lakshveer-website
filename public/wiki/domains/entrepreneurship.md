## Summary

Lakshveer Rao began his entrepreneurial journey at age 8 with his first business pitch for DIY Cars [[sig-first-pitch-2024]] and has since built multiple revenue-generating products including CircuitHeroes (300+ decks sold, ₹1,00,000 Malpani Foundation grant [[sig-malpani-grant-2025]]), a DIY eBook (100+ sales [[sig-ebook-launch-2024]]), and AI agent systems (OpenClaw/ZeroClaw [[sig-autoclaw-system-2026]]). By age 9, he became the youngest founder ever selected for The Residency Delta-2 accelerator cohort [[sig-delta2-residency-2025]], pitched at South Park Commons [[sig-spc-demo-2025]], and secured an AI Grants India grant [[sig-ai-grants-india-2025]].
