## Summary

Lakshveer has built autonomous AI agent systems since age 7, specializing in hardware-connected agents and grant automation. Core work includes Telegram-controlled ESP32 systems (2026-03), the Claw autonomous grant agent suite (OpenClaw/ZeroClaw, 2026-02–06), and Hardvare platform (2026-01). Recognized nationally by Financial Express and granted funding by AI Grants India (2025-09). Demonstrated agents at Gemini 3 Hackathon (Cerebral Valley × Google DeepMind, 2026-02) and AI Collective Hyderabad (2025-07).
