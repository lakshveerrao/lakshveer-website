# hardware
*Domain*

## Summary

Lakshveer Rao has been building physical hardware systems since age 4, starting with basic electronics (DC motor fan, 2022-08) and progressing to complex AI-integrated devices. Core competencies span PCB design, embedded systems (ESP32, Raspberry Pi), robotics, and assistive technology. By age 8, he is recognized as India's youngest hardware startup founder, shipping commercial products through Projects by Laksh, including CircuitHeroes educational card game (300+ decks sold) and the Hardvare intelligence platform.
