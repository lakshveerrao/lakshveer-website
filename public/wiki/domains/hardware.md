# hardware

## Summary
Lakshveer began building hardware at age 4 with a DC motor fan ([[sig-first-dc-motor-2022]]) and has since become India's youngest hardware startup founder. His work spans PCB design, embedded systems (ESP32, Raspberry Pi), robotics, and assistive technology — with multiple hackathon wins, media recognition, and commercial products shipped through Projects by Laksh.
