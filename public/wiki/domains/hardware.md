# hardware
*Domain*

## Summary

Lakshveer Rao has been building hardware since age 4, starting with a DC motor fan in August 2022 [[sig-first-dc-motor-2022]]. By age 8, he is recognized as India's youngest hardware startup founder through Projects by Laksh, creating physical products that combine electronics, AI agents, and computer vision. He has shipped 300+ CircuitHeroes card game decks, competed as the youngest participant in multiple hardware hackathons, and signed a co-creation agreement with Lion Circuits in November 2025 [[sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025]].

## Trajectory

**2022–2024: Foundation**
Age 4–6. First DC motor fan build (Aug 2022). Early electronics experimentation documented on YouTube.

**2024–2025: Product Launch**
Age 6–7. CircuitHeroes card game designed and launched (Oct 2024). First public demos and regional media coverage. Entered hackathons as youngest participant.

**2025–2026: Scale & Recognition**
Age 7–8. Malpani Foundation grant (Jun 2025). Lion Circuits co-creation agreement (Nov 2025). Multiple hackathon placements. National media coverage as "India's youngest hardware startup founder." Expanded into AI agents, computer vision, and assistive technology. Demonstrated at South Park Commons. Featured by Runable, Beats in Brief, StartupPedia, and LinkedIn tech networks.
