# hardware
*Domain*

## Summary
Lakshveer Rao has built hardware projects since age 4, spanning educational electronics, assistive devices, and AI-controlled embedded systems. Primary work includes CircuitHeroes (educational card game, 300+ decks sold), Drishtikon Yantra (Raspberry Pi vision device for visually impaired), and ESP32-based AI agents controllable via Telegram messaging. Recognized with Malpani Foundation Grant (2025-06) and PARAM Award (2025-10).

## Evidence
**CircuitHeroes** — Educational electronics card game funded by Malpani Foundation grant [sig-malpani-grant-2025]. 300+ decks sold by 2025-06. Presented at South Park Commons pitch demo 2025-08 [sig-spc-demo-2025].

**Drishtikon Yantra** — Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive device using computer vision and text-to-speech to describe surroundings to visually impaired users [sig-drishtikon-2026]. Demonstrated at South Park Commons 2025-08 [sig-spc-demo-2025]. Active by 2026-01.

**ESP32 AI Agent** — Telegram-controlled hardware agent capable of self-coding and device control [sig-startuppedia-2026] [sig-rediminds-linkedin-2026] [sig-somi-ai-linkedin-2026]. Featured across multiple media outlets and YouTube demonstrations 2026-03 [sig-yt-short-telegram-2026] [sig-yt-short-2-2026] [sig-yt-full-2026].

**Recognition** — PARAM Award for young innovation in STEM/hardware 2025-10 [sig-param-award-2025]. Featured in Beats in Brief covering maker journey from age 4 [sig-beats-in-brief-2025].
