## Summary

Lakshveer Rao creates educational products targeting children and makers. His approach combines hands-on learning with entrepreneurship — selling 300+ CircuitHeroes card game decks and 100+ DIY eBooks by age 16. Educational innovation earned him a ₹1,00,000 Malpani Foundation grant (2025-06).

## Evidence

**CircuitHeroes Card Game** — Educational electronics card game launched 2024-10. Teaches circuit concepts through gameplay. 300+ decks sold. Positioned as "India's youngest card game designer" [[sig-circuitheroes-launch-2024]]. Grant-funded by Malpani Foundation for educational innovation [[sig-malpani-grant-2025]].

**The Kids Book of Creative Ideas** — DIY eBook launched 2024-09 via ChhotaCreator.com. 100+ sales. Content focus: creative project ideas for children [[sig-ebook-launch-2024]].

**Learning Philosophy** — ThinkTac described him as "Grade 2 student who loves exploring how things work" (2025-06) — suggests autodidactic approach, hands-on experimentation [[sig-think-tac-feature-2025]].
