## Summary

Lakshveer Rao has been creating technical content since age 4, building a multi-format portfolio spanning YouTube (170+ project videos), eBook publishing, and short-form AI demonstrations. His content documents hardware builds, teaches electronics/robotics concepts to young makers, and has attracted formal recognition including a ₹40,000 creator micro-scholarship from Ramsri Goutham [[sig-ramsri-microscholarship-2025]]. Content serves dual purpose: education platform and brand-building mechanism for CircuitHeroes ventures.
