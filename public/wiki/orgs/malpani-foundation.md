# Malpani Foundation
*Organization*

## Summary
The Malpani Foundation is a grant-making organization that supports educational innovation and entrepreneurship in India. In June 2025, they awarded Lakshveer Rao a ₹1,00,000 grant for CircuitHeroes, recognizing the educational impact of his electronics card game.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
The foundation identified Lakshveer's CircuitHeroes project — an educational electronics card game with 300+ decks sold — as worthy of significant financial support. The ₹100,000 grant in 2025 validated his work in hardware education and entrepreneurship at age 16. This represents institutional recognition of his maker education initiatives.
