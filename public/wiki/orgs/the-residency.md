# The Residency
*Organization*

## Summary
The Residency is an entrepreneurship accelerator program that operates cohort-based programs for early-stage founders. Their Delta Chapter program accepts founders into structured cohorts. In September 2025, Lakshveer Rao was selected for Delta Chapter II (Delta-2), making him the youngest founder ever accepted into the program at age 8.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer was accepted into The Residency's Delta Chapter II cohort in September 2025 [[sig-delta2-residency-2025]]. The organization publicly announced him as "the youngest founder ever currently in our Delta Chapter II cohort" via their official LinkedIn channel. By January 2026, Lakshveer was still participating in the delta cohort when Yuvraj Aaditya met him and noted his status as "the youngest in @theresidency delta cohort" [[sig-aadityayuvraj-residency-2026]].
