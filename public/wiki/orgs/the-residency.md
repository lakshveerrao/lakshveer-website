# The Residency
*Organization*

## Summary
The Residency is a startup accelerator program that operates cohort-based programs for young founders. Their Delta-2 cohort (2025-09) selected Lakshveer Rao as the youngest founder ever admitted to the program at age 8/9.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer was accepted into The Residency's Delta Chapter II cohort in September 2025, making him the youngest founder in the program's history. The organization publicly highlighted this achievement via LinkedIn. By January 2026, Lakshveer was meeting with other cohort members including Yuvraj Aaditya, who acknowledged his builder credentials spanning hardware, software, and games [[sig-aadityayuvraj-residency-2026]].
