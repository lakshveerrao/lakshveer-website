# Runable
*Organization*

## Summary
Runable is a maker community organization that hosts hardware and AI hackathons, most notably the **RunTogether Hackathon** in January 2026. The organization gained attention for its inclusive builder culture after an 8-year-old participant (Lakshveer Rao) built a motion-controlled gaming system at their event. Runable operates the X handle @runable_hq and appears focused on democratizing hardware/AI prototyping.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao participated in Runable's **RunTogether Hackathon** (January 2026) at age 8, where he built **MotionX** — a full-body motion-control gaming system using computer vision and gesture recognition [[sig-motionx-launch-2026]]. The project won **Special Mention** and became a case study for the organization's philosophy. Runable's official X account tweeted: *"An 8-year-old showed up and built. That alone changed what we thought was possible."* [[sig-runable-runhq-tweet-2026]]. Multiple testimonials from participants (Umesh Kumar [[sig-itsumeshk-runtogether-2026]], founder Eshaan Pawan [[sig-eshaanpawan-runtogether-2026]]) cited Lakshveer's participation as redefining expectations for "builder" identity.
