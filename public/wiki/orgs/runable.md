# Runable
*Organization*

## Summary
Runable is a technology organization that hosted the **RunTogether Hackathon** in January 2026, where participants built AI and hardware projects. The organization gained visibility after featuring an 8-year-old builder (Lakshveer Rao) who developed a motion-control gaming system at their event. Runable operates under the social handle `@runable_hq` and is associated with `@scaletogether`.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao participated in Runable's **RunTogether Hackathon** (January 2026) and built **MotionX**, a full-body motion-control gaming system using computer vision and AI. His project won **Special Mention Winner** status. Runable publicly featured Lakshveer's participation in a tweet stating *"An 8-year-old showed up and built. That alone changed what we thought was possible."* [[sig-runable-runhq-tweet-2026]]. The organization's founder, Eshaan Pawan, later stated that Lakshveer's participation *"changed what we thought builder meant"* [[sig-eshaanpawan-runtogether-2026]].
