# PCB Cupid
*Organization*

## Summary
PCB Cupid is a hardware and electronics organization that co-organized Hardware Hackathon 2.0 with Lion Circuits in July 2025. The organization focuses on printed circuit board (PCB) design and electronics manufacturing within the Indian hardware ecosystem.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao competed in Hardware Hackathon 2.0, co-organized by PCB Cupid and Lion Circuits, finishing as a Top-5 Finalist and the youngest participant [sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]. This was his second consecutive appearance at Lion Circuits hardware events after participating in Hardware Hackathon 1.0 earlier that month [sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025].
