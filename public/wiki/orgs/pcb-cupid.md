# PCB Cupid
*Organization*

## Summary
PCB Cupid is a hardware organization that co-organized Hardware Hackathon 2.0 with Lion Circuits in July 2025. The organization operates in the electronics and hardware maker space, hosting competitive technical events for builders and engineers.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao participated in Hardware Hackathon 2.0, co-organized by PCB Cupid and Lion Circuits, where he placed as a Top-5 Finalist and was the youngest participant at 8 years old [[sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]]. This was his second consecutive appearance at a Lion Circuits hardware hackathon series.
