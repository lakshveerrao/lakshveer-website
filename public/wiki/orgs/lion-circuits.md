# Lion Circuits
*Organization — PCB Manufacturing & Hardware Events*

## Summary

Lion Circuits is a PCB (Printed Circuit Board) manufacturing company based in India that organizes hardware hackathons and maker events. In November 2025, the company signed a co-creation agreement with 8-year-old Lakshveer Rao, formalizing a business relationship with the young hardware maker. Lion Circuits hosts the Hardware Hackathon series in collaboration with PCB Cupid.

## Relationship with Lakshveer

Lion Circuits maintains a formal business relationship with Lakshveer through a **co-creation agreement signed in November 2025** [sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025]. This partnership positions Lakshveer as a collaborative creator with the PCB manufacturer.

Lakshveer first engaged with Lion Circuits at **Hardware Hackathon 1.0 (July 2025)**, where he was the youngest participant and demonstrated a hydration assistant built with a Glyph board and sensor [sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025]. He returned for **Hardware Hackathon 2.0 (July 2025)**, placing in the **Top-5 finalists** as the youngest participant [sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025].
