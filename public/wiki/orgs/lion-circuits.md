# Lion Circuits
*Organization*

## Summary
Lion Circuits is a hardware and electronics organization that hosts hackathons focused on PCB design and embedded systems. The organization collaborated with PCB Cupid to run Hardware Hackathon 1.0 and 2.0 in July 2025, featuring young makers and hardware enthusiasts.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer participated in two consecutive Lion Circuits hackathons in July 2025. At Hardware Hackathon 1.0, he competed as the youngest participant, building a hydration assistant using a Glyph board and sensor [sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025]. He returned for Hardware Hackathon 2.0, where he placed as a Top-5 Finalist and again was the youngest participant in the competition [sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]. Both events were organized in collaboration with PCB Cupid.
