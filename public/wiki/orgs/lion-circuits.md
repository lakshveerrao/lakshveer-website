# Lion Circuits
*Organization*

## Summary

Lion Circuits is a PCB manufacturing company based in India that hosts hardware hackathons and supports young makers in the electronics ecosystem. The organization signed a co-creation agreement with Lakshveer Rao in November 2025, following his participation as the youngest competitor in their Hardware Hackathon series during July 2025.

## Relationship with Lakshveer

Lion Circuits has been a key institutional supporter of Lakshveer's hardware journey. He participated in **Hardware Hackathon 1.0** (July 2025) as the youngest participant, building a hydration assistant using a Glyph board and sensor [[sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025]]. The organization publicly recognized his work, stating "An 8-year-old just schooled us all."

At **Hardware Hackathon 2.0** (July 2025), Lakshveer placed as a **Top-5 Finalist**, again as the youngest participant [[sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]]. These events were co-organized with PCB Cupid.

In **November 2025**, Lion Circuits formalized their relationship by signing a **co-creation agreement** with Lakshveer [[sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025]]. This represents one of the first known commercial partnerships between an 8-year-old hardware founder and a PCB manufacturing company.
