# Lion Circuits
*Organization*

## Summary
Lion Circuits is a PCB manufacturing company based in India that organizes hardware hackathons and supports young makers. The company signed a co-creation agreement with Lakshveer Rao in November 2025, following his participation in their Hardware Hackathon series where he was the youngest participant and achieved Top-5 Finalist status.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lion Circuits first engaged with Lakshveer at Hardware Hackathon 1.0 (July 2025), where the 8-year-old built a hydration assistant using a Glyph board and sensor, becoming the youngest participant. [sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025] He returned for Hardware Hackathon 2.0, again as the youngest participant, and placed as a Top-5 Finalist. [sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]

In November 2025, Lion Circuits formalized their relationship by signing a co-creation agreement with Lakshveer. [sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025] This partnership represents institutional recognition of his hardware capabilities at age 8.
