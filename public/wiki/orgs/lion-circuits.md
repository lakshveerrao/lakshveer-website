# Lion Circuits
*Organization*

## Summary
Lion Circuits is an Indian PCB manufacturing company that organizes hardware hackathons and collaborates with young makers. The organization runs competitive hardware events (Hardware Hackathon 1.0 and 2.0) in partnership with PCB Cupid, and has established formal co-creation agreements with emerging hardware talent.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lion Circuits signed a co-creation agreement with Lakshveer in November 2025 [sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025]. Prior to this formal partnership, Lakshveer participated in two Lion Circuits hackathons in July 2025. At Hardware Hackathon 1.0, he was the youngest participant and built a hydration assistant using a Glyph board and sensor [sig-lion-circuits-hw1-2025]. At Hardware Hackathon 2.0, he placed as a Top-5 Finalist, again as the youngest participant [sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]. His participation generated social media attention from industry figures including Murali Srinivasa [sig-muralisrinivasa-hwhackathon-2025] and Yasser MuadDib [sig-muaddibayasser-hwhackathon-2025].
