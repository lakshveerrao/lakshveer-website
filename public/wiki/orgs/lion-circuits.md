# Lion Circuits
*Organization*

## Summary
Lion Circuits is a PCB manufacturing company based in India that organizes hardware hackathons and collaborates with young makers. The organization partners with PCB Cupid to run competitive hardware events and maintains co-creation agreements with emerging hardware developers.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer has competed in two Lion Circuits hardware hackathons (2025-07) as the youngest participant in both events, placing Top-5 at Hardware Hackathon 2.0 [sig-lion-circuits-hw2-2025]. In November 2025, he signed a formal co-creation agreement with Lion Circuits [sig-lion-circuits-cocreation-2025], establishing an ongoing partnership for PCB-based hardware projects.
