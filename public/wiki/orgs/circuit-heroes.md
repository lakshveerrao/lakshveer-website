# Circuit Heroes
*Organization*

## Summary
Circuit Heroes is an electronics education card game created by Lakshveer Rao (age 8) and his father, turning real-world electronic components into playable learning tools. The product launched on Amazon by April 2025 and has been recognized by builders, educators, and maker communities for introducing children to hardware concepts through gamification.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Circuit Heroes is Lakshveer's primary product venture under his broader "Projects by Laksh" brand. He is credited as co-founder alongside his father (CaptVenk). The product represents Lakshveer's applied approach to hardware education — building tools that teach others what he's learning. By March 2026, he was publicly identified as India's youngest hardware startup founder primarily due to Circuit Heroes [[sig-dileep-gupta-linkedin-2026]].
