# Circuit Heroes
*Organization · Educational Product*

## Summary

Circuit Heroes is an educational card game that teaches electronics components and circuits, created by Lakshveer Rao (then age 8) and his father. The product is sold on Amazon and designed to introduce children to electronics through gameplay. As of 2025-2026, Circuit Heroes has gained recognition in the Indian maker and EdTech communities as a "hardware startup" and educational tool.

## Relationship with Lakshveer

Circuit Heroes is Lakshveer Rao's co-founded venture with his father (CaptVenk). Dileep Gupta identified Lakshveer at age 8 as "India's youngest hardware startup founder" based on Circuit Heroes [[sig-dileep-gupta-linkedin-2026]]. The product represents Lakshveer's first commercial product launch, distributed through Amazon's marketplace [[sig-shantanugoel-circuitheroes-buy-2025]]. Lakshveer actively demonstrated Circuit Heroes to institutional partners including the principal of Manchester International School [[sig-manchester-school-principal-2025]].
