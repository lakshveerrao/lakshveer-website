# Circuit Heroes
*Organization*

## Summary

Circuit Heroes is an educational hardware product created by Lakshveer Rao to teach children about electronics components through gameplay. The product — a card game that introduces real-world electronic components in a playable format — is sold on Amazon and has been featured by education-focused brands. Circuit Heroes represents Lakshveer's first commercial product launch, positioning him as India's youngest hardware startup founder as of 2026-03.

## Relationship with Lakshveer

Circuit Heroes is the flagship product of [[Lakshveer Rao]]'s hardware entrepreneurship. Lakshveer created the card game as a method to make electronics education accessible to children. The product is co-developed with his father (referred to as "CaptVenk" in public documentation), forming a "father-son duo" that has been building and documenting the venture publicly. As of 2025-04, Circuit Heroes is commercially available on Amazon. By 2026-03, industry observers identified Lakshveer as "India's youngest hardware startup founder" in connection with this venture [[sig-dileep-gupta-linkedin-2026]].

The product has been used in educational demonstrations — notably when the principal of Manchester International School learned about Circuit Heroes directly from Lakshveer in 2025-03 [[sig-manchester-school-principal-2025]].
