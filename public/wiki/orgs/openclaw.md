# OpenClaw
*Organization*

## Summary
OpenClaw is an autonomous AI agent system built by [[Lakshveer Rao]] for automated grant discovery and application. The system operates as part of the broader Claw AI infrastructure, working alongside ZeroClaw to handle funding opportunities autonomously. Public recognition came via CaptVenk's social media coverage in June 2026.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer is the founder and builder of OpenClaw. He developed the system as part of his Claw AI agent infrastructure in February 2026, when he was 8 years old. OpenClaw represents his entry into autonomous agent development focused on entrepreneurial funding workflows.
