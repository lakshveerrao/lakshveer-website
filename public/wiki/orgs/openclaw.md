# OpenClaw
*Organization*

## Summary
OpenClaw is an autonomous AI agent system built by Lakshveer Rao for grant discovery and application. Part of the broader "Claw" AI system (which includes ZeroClaw), OpenClaw operates as an autonomous grant agent that identifies and applies for funding opportunities. The system was operational by February 2026 and gained visibility through social channels by June 2026.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao is the founder and builder of OpenClaw. He developed the system as part of his Claw AI agent portfolio at age 8, demonstrating advanced capabilities in autonomous agent design and entrepreneurial funding automation.
