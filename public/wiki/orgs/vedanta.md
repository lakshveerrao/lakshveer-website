# Vedanta
*Organization*

## Summary
Vedanta is a corporate sponsor and partner organization that co-hosted a makeathon with Param Foundation in October 2025. The organization facilitated recognition of young innovators working on social impact hardware projects. Lakshveer demonstrated work to spiritual leader Sri Sri Brahmananda Bharati Swamiji through Vedanta connections in February 2026.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Co-sponsored the Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon (October 2025) where Lakshveer won Youngest Innovator and Special Mention awards for [[Drishtikon Yantra]] — a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device for visually impaired users. Facilitated Lakshveer's demonstration to His Holiness Sri Sri Brahmananda Bharati Swamiji (February 2026), documented via YouTube.
