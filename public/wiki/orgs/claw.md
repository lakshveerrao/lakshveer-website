# Claw
*Organization*

## Summary
Claw is an AI agent system founded by Lakshveer Rao in early 2026. The platform deploys autonomous agents for grant discovery and application, with two documented subsystems: OpenClaw (grant-finding agent) and ZeroClaw. As of June 2026, the system has gained attention in entrepreneurship and AI agent communities.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao is the founder and builder of the Claw AI system. He developed the architecture and deployed both OpenClaw and ZeroClaw agents as part of his entrepreneurial portfolio at age 8.
