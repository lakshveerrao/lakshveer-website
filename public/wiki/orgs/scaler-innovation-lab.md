# Scaler Innovation Lab
*Organization*

## Summary

Scaler Innovation Lab is the institutional entity associated with Scaler School of Technology (Scaler SST), operating hackathons and tech festivals in India. In September 2025, the lab hosted a major hackathon drawing 200 teams and organized Yugaantar 2025, a student-led tech festival that received national press coverage from Financial Express and Jagran Josh.

## Relationship with Lakshveer

Lakshveer Rao competed in the Scaler Innovation Lab hackathon in September 2025, placing in the top 8 out of 200 teams [[sig-scaler-hackathon-2025]]. He also participated in Yugaantar 2025, the institution's student-led tech festival [[sig-jagran-josh-yugaantar-2025]] [[sig-fe-scaler-2025]].
