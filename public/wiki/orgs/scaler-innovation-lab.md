# Scaler Innovation Lab
*Organization*

## Summary
Scaler Innovation Lab is the innovation and events arm of Scaler School of Technology (Scaler SST), an Indian technology education institution. The organization hosts large-scale hackathons and student-led tech festivals, including Yugaantar 2025, which drew national media coverage from Financial Express and Jagran Josh in September 2025.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao competed in the Scaler Innovation Lab hackathon in September 2025, placing in the top 8 out of 200 participating teams [[sig-scaler-hackathon-2025]]. He also participated in Yugaantar 2025, the student-led tech festival organized by Scaler School of Technology [[sig-jagran-josh-yugaantar-2025]], [[sig-fe-scaler-2025]].
