# Scaler Innovation Lab
*Organization*

## Summary
Scaler Innovation Lab is the innovation and entrepreneurship arm of Scaler School of Technology (Scaler SST), a technology education institution in India. The organization hosts hackathons, tech festivals, and student-led innovation programs. In September 2025, it organized a major hackathon attracting 200+ teams and the Yugaantar 2025 tech fest, both receiving national press coverage.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer competed in the Scaler Innovation Lab Hackathon in September 2025, reaching the top 8 out of 200 participating teams [[sig-scaler-hackathon-2025]]. He also participated in Yugaantar 2025, the student-led tech festival organized by Scaler SST, which was covered by Jagran Josh [[sig-jagran-josh-yugaantar-2025]] and Financial Express [[sig-fe-scaler-2025]].
