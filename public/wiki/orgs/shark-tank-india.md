# Shark Tank India
*Organization*

## Summary
Shark Tank India is the Indian franchise of the global reality television series where entrepreneurs pitch their business ideas to a panel of investor-judges ("Sharks"). The show airs on Sony LIV and provides startups with potential funding, mentorship, and brand visibility. Season 5 ran selection processes in mid-2025.
