# Projects by Laksh
*Organization*

## Summary

**Projects by Laksh** is Lakshveer Rao's hardware startup and YouTube-based maker education platform, established 2022. Started as a YouTube channel documenting DIY electronics builds at age 4, evolved into India's youngest hardware startup with 170+ documented projects spanning electronics, robotics, and AI agents. Ships physical products including CircuitHeroes educational card game and DIY eBooks.

## Relationship with Lakshveer

Lakshveer Rao is co-founder and primary creator. Functions as content platform (YouTube: @ProjectsByLaksh), product brand (CircuitHeroes, eBooks), and business entity for pitches and press. All builds, tutorials, and products originate from Lakshveer's work.
