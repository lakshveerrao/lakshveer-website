# Junicorns
*Organization*

## Summary
Junicorns is a competition program for young founders, operated in connection with India Startup Festival (ISF). The program identifies and supports early-stage youth entrepreneurship through a multi-level selection process.

## Relationship with Lakshveer
Lakshveer Rao was shortlisted to Level 3 of the ISF Junicorns competition in August 2025, representing recognition of his work as a hardware founder [[sig-isf-junicorns-2025]].
