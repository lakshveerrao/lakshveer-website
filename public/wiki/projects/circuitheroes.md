# CircuitHeroes
*Project*

## Summary
CircuitHeroes is an educational electronics card game designed by Lakshveer Rao, making him India's youngest card game designer. Launched in October 2024, the game teaches electronics concepts through gameplay and has sold 300+ decks as of June 2025. The project secured a ₹1,00,000 grant from Malpani Foundation and has been showcased at multiple national platforms.
