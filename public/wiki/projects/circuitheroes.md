# CircuitHeroes
*Project*

## Summary
CircuitHeroes is an educational electronics card game designed by Lakshveer Rao, launched in October 2024. The game teaches electronics concepts through gameplay, positioning Lakshveer as India's youngest card game designer. As of June 2025, the project had sold 300+ decks and received a ₹1,00,000 grant from the Malpani Foundation.
