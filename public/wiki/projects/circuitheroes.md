# CircuitHeroes
*Project*

## Summary

CircuitHeroes is an educational electronics card game designed and launched by Lakshveer Rao in October 2024. The game teaches fundamental electronics concepts through gameplay, positioning Lakshveer as India's youngest card game designer. As of June 2025, CircuitHeroes had sold 300+ decks and secured ₹1,00,000 in grant funding from the Malpani Foundation.
