# CircuitHeroes
*Project*

## Summary
CircuitHeroes is an educational electronics card game designed to teach circuit concepts through gameplay. Developed by Lakshveer Rao, the project has sold 300+ decks and received recognition through the Malpani Foundation grant in June 2025. The game represents a bridge between education and hands-on hardware learning for young makers.

## Status & Timeline
- **2025-06**: Awarded Malpani Foundation grant for CircuitHeroes innovation
- **2025-08**: Demonstrated CircuitHeroes at South Park Commons pitch demo in Bangalore alongside [[drishtikon-yantra]]
- **2025-09**: Featured in Beats in Brief media coverage as part of Lakshveer's maker journey
- **Sales milestone**: 300+ decks sold as of June 2025
