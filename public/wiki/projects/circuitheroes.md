# CircuitHeroes
*Project*

## Summary

CircuitHeroes is an educational electronics card game designed by Lakshveer Rao at age 8, positioning him as India's youngest card game designer. Launched in October 2024, the game teaches electronics concepts through gameplay and has achieved commercial validation with 300+ decks sold and recognition from the Malpani Foundation via a ₹1,00,000 grant in June 2025.
