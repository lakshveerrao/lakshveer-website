# CircuitHeroes
*Project*

## Summary
CircuitHeroes is an educational electronics card game designed and launched by Lakshveer Rao in October 2024, making him India's youngest card game designer at age 14-15. The game teaches electronics concepts through gameplay and has sold 300+ decks as of June 2025, generating commercial traction and institutional recognition including a ₹1,00,000 grant from Malpani Foundation.
