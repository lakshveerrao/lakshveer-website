# Drishtikon Yantra
*Project*

## Summary
Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device designed for visually impaired users. Lakshveer developed and publicly demonstrated the device in 2025, winning Special Mention at the Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon (2025-10) as Youngest Innovator. The device represents Lakshveer's work in accessible hardware and computer vision applications.
