# Drishtikon Yantra
*Project*

## Summary
Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device for visually impaired users. Developed by Lakshveer Rao and publicly demonstrated at South Park Commons (2025-08) and Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon (2025-10), where it received Special Mention and Youngest Innovator awards.
