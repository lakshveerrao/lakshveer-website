# Drishtikon Yantra
*Project*

## Summary
Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device for visually impaired users, developed by Lakshveer Rao. The device leverages computer vision to provide environmental awareness. Publicly demonstrated August 2025 and recognized with Special Mention at Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon October 2025.
