# MotionX
*Project*

## Summary
MotionX is a full-body motion-control gaming system built by Lakshveer Rao at the RunTogether Hackathon in January 2026. The system uses computer vision and AI for gesture recognition, enabling controller-free gaming through body movements. MotionX won Special Mention at the hackathon and became a viral moment when Runable HQ tweeted about an 8-year-old builder changing their perception of what's possible.

## Status & Timeline
- **2026-01**: Built and demoed at RunTogether Hackathon hosted by Runable
- **2026-01**: Won Special Mention award
- **2026-01**: Featured by @runable_hq and @scaletogether on X (Twitter)

## Technical Architecture
- Computer vision-based motion tracking
- AI-powered gesture recognition
- Full-body movement detection (no controllers)
- Real-time gaming integration
