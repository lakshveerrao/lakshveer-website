# Drishtikon
*Project*

## Summary
Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device developed by Lakshveer Rao for visually impaired users. The hardware project integrates computer vision capabilities into a wearable form factor. Lakshveer has publicly presented the device at multiple venues and received recognition at the Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon in October 2025.
