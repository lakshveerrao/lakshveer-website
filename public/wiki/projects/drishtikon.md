# Drishtikon
*Project*

## Summary
Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive device that uses computer vision and text-to-speech to describe the surrounding environment to visually impaired users. Lakshveer presented the project at South Park Commons in Bangalore (2025-08). The device represents his work in accessibility technology and social impact hardware.

## Technical Architecture
- **Hardware**: Raspberry Pi-based wearable form factor
- **Vision Stack**: Computer vision for environmental analysis
- **Output**: Text-to-speech synthesis for audio feedback
- **Target Users**: Visually impaired individuals

## Impact

- **Approach**: Low-cost assistive device using commodity hardware
