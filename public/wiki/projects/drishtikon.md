# Drishtikon
*Project*

## Summary
Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device designed for visually impaired users. Developed by Lakshveer Rao in 2025, the device uses computer vision to provide environmental awareness. Won Special Mention at Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon (2025-10).
