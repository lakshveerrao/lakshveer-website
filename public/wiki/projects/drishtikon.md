# Drishtikon
*Project*

## Summary
Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device designed for visually impaired users. The hardware prototype uses computer vision to provide real-time environmental feedback. Lakshveer developed and publicly demoed the project in 2025, earning recognition at the Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon.
