# Drishtikon
*Project*

## Summary

Drishtikon Yantra is a Raspberry Pi-based wearable assistive vision device designed for visually impaired users. Developed by Lakshveer Rao, the device uses computer vision to provide environmental awareness and navigation assistance. The project won Special Mention and Youngest Innovator awards at the Param Foundation × Vedanta Makeathon in October 2025.
